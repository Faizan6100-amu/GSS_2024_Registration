The Global Sustainability Summit (GSS) 2024
Registration Form

The Global Sustainability Summit (GSS) 2024 is an Annual Flagship Global Program of IISD-CMI to showcase India's Global Leadership in the field of Sustainability and Decarbonization, Since both Institutes have completed over 16 years of Dedicated Services to the Nation and the Planet. IISD-CMI are Proud National Public Policy think tanks and Scientific Research Organizations based in New Delhi. They work with the Central and State Governments in India, UN Member States, International Scientific Laboratories, a network of Credible Universities, leading Diplomats and Policymakers, Industry and Business Leaders, Academicians, Women and Youth, Global thought Leaders, and Prolific Thinkers around the World.
